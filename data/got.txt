Cersei Lannister is queen regent of the Seven Kingdoms.
She is the daughter of Tywin Lannister.
Tyrion Lannister is her younger brother.
Jaime Lannister is her twin brother.

Cersei was born at Casterly Rock.
She was married to King Robert Baratheon.
Cersei has three children: Joffrey, Myrcella, and Tommen.

Her children are secretly fathered by her twin brother Jaime, not Robert.
Cersei is known for her cunning, ambition, and fierce protection of her children.
After Robert's death, she rules as queen regent for her son Joffrey.

Her father Tywin Lannister is the most powerful lord in Westeros.
Cersei deeply resents being a woman in a world dominated by men.
She was prophecied by Maggy the Frog that all her children would die before her.

Cersei eventually drinks wildfire to destroy the Great Sept of Baelor, eliminating her enemies.
She becomes Queen of the Seven Kingdoms after Tommen's death.
Ned Stark was the Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North.

He was executed on the orders of King Joffrey Baratheon.
Ser Ilyn Payne carried out the execution at the Great Sept of Baelor.
The Iron Throne is the seat of power of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros.
It was forged from the swords of defeated enemies by Aegon the Conqueror.
