The "Dark Passenger": Dexter is a psychopath with a compulsive urge to kill, which he refers to as his "Dark Passenger".
Double Life: He masks his true nature by acting as a normal human, complete with a job, a girlfriend (later wife, Rita Bennett), and a relationship with his sister, Debra Morgan.

Origin: As a child, he witnessed his mother's brutal murder, which created his traumatic, violent tendencies.
Modus Operandi: He kills using a ritualistic method, often involving plastic-wrapped rooms, to avoid leaving evidence, making him a "functioning" serial killer who rarely gets caught.

Dexter faked his death by staging a boating accident during a hurricane.
He did this to protect his son Harrison and his girlfriend Hannah from danger.
After faking his death, Dexter assumed a new identity as a lumberjack in Oregon.
